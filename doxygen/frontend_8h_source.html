00009 ** Introduction
00010 ** -----------------------
00011 ** This file describes a means to easily communicate to Winamp
00012 ** via the classic Win32 Message API. 
00013 **
00014 ** These definitions/code assume C/C++. Porting to VB/Delphi shouldn't
00015 ** be too hard.
00016 ** 
00017 ** First, you find the HWND of the Winamp main window. From a plug-in
00018 ** you can easily extract this from the plug-in structure (hMainWindow,
00019 ** hwndParent, whatever). For external apps, use:
00020 **
00021 ** HWND hwnd_winamp = FindWindow("Winamp v1.x",NULL);
00022 **
00023 ** (note: I know, we're in Winamp 2.x, but it's 1.x for compatibility)
00024 **
00025 ** Once you have the hwnd_winamp, it's a good idea to check the version 
00026 ** number. To do this, you send a WM_WA_IPC message to hwnd_winamp.
00027 ** Note that WM_WA_IPC is defined as Win32's WM_USER.
00028 **
00029 ** Note that sometimes you might want to use PostMessage instead of
00030 ** SendMessage.
00031 */
